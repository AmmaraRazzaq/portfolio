About Me

I did bachelors in Electrical Engineering with majors in computer science from University of Engineering and Technology Lahore, Pakistan. Then I moved to UAE and did my masters in Computer Science from University of Sharjah. I worked as a research assistant in my department and published one conference and one journal paper. My interests include data analysis and visualization and machine learning. I earned different certificates in data science and have worked on several projects related to machine learning and data science.

I actively participate in entrepreneurial competitions and hackathons. Recently I participated in UAE Hackathon and was selected as a finalist in Rasalkhaima hackathon. I make time to attend tech related events and workshops such as AI Everything. These events are a huge oppurtunity for learning about new technology and showcasing your abilities to make an impact in the community.
